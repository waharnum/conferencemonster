The OLA Board launches the strategic plan 2014 – 2017!

Thank you to all in library-land who contributed your 
ideas to the process last spring. Your input confirmed our 
existing vision of “all Ontarians are free to imagine, learn 
and discover, and recognize and celebrate library and 
information services as an essential resource for realizing 
individual aspirations and developing communities.”

Our four strategic priorities supporting our mission remain 
relevant but the projects and plans aligned to each priority 
are brand new. Visit OLA’s website to learn more about 
what’s in store! Here’s a snapshot:

Growing career paths and potential:

 delivering new 

Professional Development opportunities, a student 
engagement plan, strengthening opportunities for 
volunteers, and more. 

Collaborating to Extend Libraries’ Strategic Voice: 

with key 

library partner organizations, ramping up value, impact, 
positioning (VIP) of libraries, government relations, and a 
province wide collaboration on marketing and showcasing 
libraries.

Strengthening the Organization: 

inter-divisional 

collaboration on critical library issues, reviewing our 
governance, delivering value to leadership to support 
association involvement, providing a platform for members 
to identify interests and areas of expertise.

Transforming Ideas into Solutions: 

the transformation of 

Super Conference – we love it but we want to blow your 
minds – what else can Super Conference be?

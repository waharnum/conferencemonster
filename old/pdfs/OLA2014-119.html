Liz Kerr, our Super Conference champion

More than 25 OLA members spend a year on the Super Conference planning 
team designing the ultimate educational and networking experience for the 
library world. Unobtrusively and behind the scenes is a one-woman conductor 
who enthusiastically, energetically, and most diplomatically weaves the process 
together. Liz, when you retire at the end of February, we will miss your humour, 
your tact, and your extraordinary ability to curate for quality that ensured our 
conference remained “super”!

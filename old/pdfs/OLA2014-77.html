DATA MINE (ING):  
THE QUANTIFIED SELF, 
TECHNOLOGY, & LIBRARIES

Shelley Archibald, Trainer, 
Burlington PL

Self-knowledge through 
numbers goes by several 
names: self-tracking, body 
data, and life hacking. This 
phenomena uses web and 
mobile technology to help 
individuals track data in many 
areas of their life: from health 
concerns, to sleep patterns, 
fitness regimes, and moods. 
The goal of this endeavour 
is to empower individuals to 
change their lives for the better 
based on an analysis of the 
data accumulated. Learn the 
Quantified Self Movement and 
explore where libraries fit into 
this new realm of information 
technology.

Margot Stornelli, Dufferin-Peel 
Catholic DSB

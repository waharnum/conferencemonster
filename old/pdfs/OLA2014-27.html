Library staff at all levels 
of an organization should 
be expected to maintain a 
culture supportive of diversity. 
A diversity plan identifies 
priority areas and presents 
recommendations designed 
to create a more inclusive 
environment for both library 
staff and users. Having a 
diversity plan in place enables 
your library to be in alignment 
with your institution’s equity, 
internationalization, and 
accessibility goals.

framework for information

Based on a library discussion, 
uWaterloo Sociology graduate 
students identified a gap 
in the realm of scholarly

of graduate student research. 
To fill the gap, the students 
envisioned, created, and 
launched the successful 
Canadian Graduate Journal

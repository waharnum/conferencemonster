Child & Youth Expo

The OPLA Child & Youth Services Committee hosts the 
5th annual EXPO celebrating services and programs for 
children, tweens, and teens in Ontario public libraries. 
The EXPO is a showcase of “best and promising 
practices” with lots of opportunity for discussion, 
networking, and gathering lots of new ideas! Some of 
the sessions featured this year include:

• Inspirations! with Monika Conduit, Brampton PL
• Hockey Night at the Library with Janelle Wenger, Family 

Literacy Co-ordinator, Region of Waterloo Library

• Storytimes: Exploring the Possibilities! with Julie Brandl, 

London PL

Vaughan PL

• Eureka! Accessible TDSRC Activities with Karen Brophy, 

CNIB Library

MAKING GOOD ON A 
PROMISE: THE CENTRE 
FOR EQUITABLE 
LIBRARY ACCESS

Andrew Martin, Senior Project 
Director, Centre for Equitable 
Library Access; Sandra Singh, CEO, 
Vancouver PL & Working Group 
Chair, CULC/CBUC’s Task Force 
on Equitable Library Access 

CULC/CBUC has been 
working on the creation of a 
new national public library 
organization focused on the 
creation, collection, delivery, 
and preservation of both 
digital and physical alternate 
format accessible materials to 
print-disabled Canadians. The 
new not-for-profit will assume 
responsibility for CNIB’s 
existing curated collection of 
materials, as well as leverage 
the extensive publicly-funded 
digital repository, ILS, and 
physical production and 
delivery infrastructure currently 
used through the CNIB Library 
Service and from existing public 
library partners.

Charlotte Meissner, Oakville PL

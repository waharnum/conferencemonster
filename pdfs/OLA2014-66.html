Pop-Up Makerspace@OLA

Kids Learning Code and MakerKids will be creating a pop-up 
makerspace on the EXPO floor! Come and see a typical layout, 
and learn some activities by trying them yourself. You’ll get to 
see toy hacking, Arduino robot rovers, Cube 3D Printers from 
3D Systems, HTML, MIT’s Scratch, and more. The activities are 
designed to be run in your own spaces, and you will be supplied 
with teaching materials to show you how! Check out their websites, 
www.makerkids.ca and www.kidslearningcode.com, and ask them 
about their Making Makers modules which they are developing 
for teachers and librarians to use to teach maker activities to kids. 
Library staff from some of the Ontario libraries already involved 
in the maker culture movement will be popping in to discuss and 
display their maker projects and programs.

MOBILIZE YOUR LIBRARY WITH BOOPSIE FOR LIBRARIES

Sam Nickell, Executive Vice President, Business 
Development, Boopsie for Libraries 

Increase circulation and patron engagement by as much as 30% by 
offering your community a custom-branded mobile app that works 
on ANY mobile phone or tablet device.
Simply pulling your catalogue into an app no longer enough to 
keep your patrons engaged. 60% of the content being accessed via 
Boopsie apps today is non-catalogue data and resources. You’ve 
invested time, energy, and money in sourcing third-party content 
and resources, and it’s imperative these are included in your 
mobile app. Boopsie integrates with many of the major providers, 
including Mango, OverDrive, 3M, and many more, providing an 
easy, one-click experience. Boopsie makes it easy for librarians to 
better serve their changing communities and even provides multi-
lingual apps!
Make your library an Icon on your patron’s mobile device so they 
have you in their pocket 24/7!

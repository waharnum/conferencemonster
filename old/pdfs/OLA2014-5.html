A UNIVERSE OF POSSIBILITIES

On behalf of the OLA Board, the conference planning committee under the leadership of Co-
Chairs, Lita Barrie and Richard Reid, along with OLA’s Executive Director, Shelagh Paterson, 
it is my honour to welcome you to the 2014 Ontario Library Association Super Conference. As 
we celebrate the 20th anniversary of this amazing conference, we also look to the future as we 
consider “A Universe of Possibilities.”

Wonder. Learn. Connect. Inspire. Collaborate. Educate. Co-operate. Imagine. Innovate. Create. 
Transform. Impact. Educate. Interact. Entertain. Empower. These are words that have been used 
in the Super Conference program the last several years to describe our passion and professional 
mandate to enrich lives, empower people, and build community through relevant, accessible 
library service. They are also words used to describe our conference experience.

Developments in the world around us are revolutionizing how people communicate, socialize, 
learn, and create. This has created a universe of possibilities for continued provision of impactful, 
vital library and information services. The 2014 Super Conference provides a “tremendous place 
to think about where we are in history” as we learn about recent innovations and best practices, 
and consider exciting possibilities for the future.

Enjoy!

Susanna Hubbard Krimmer
OLA President, 2013
CEO & Chief Librarian, London Public Library

Are you a new OLA Member?
Is this your first OLA Super Conference?
This session is for you!
Tanis Fink, OLA 2011 President, and co-
host Klara Maidenberg, Scholars Portal, 
welcome you!
The library world is a close-knit 
community where reputations and 
relationships can be important 
determinants of success. While the 
profession is generally welcoming, 
becoming a part of this community can 
feel challenging for those new to libraries. 
This diverse panel of librarians will share 
advice for smooth integration into the 
profession with helpful tips including how 
to network without being annoying, how 
to talk so potential employers will listen, 
and use your online presence to achieve 
professional goals.

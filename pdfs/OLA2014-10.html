Meeting Room Size
Session rooms are pre-assigned using the choices provided 
by delegates in the registration process. Since delegates 
are not obligated to attend the sessions selected, and 
since many do not fill in this information, a few rooms may 
prove inadequate for the demand. For comfort and safety, 
convenors are asked to close sessions that reach capacity. 
Those who are turned away have between 20 and 30 other 
sessions in the same time slot to choose from.

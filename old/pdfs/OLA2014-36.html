EBOOKS, PUBLISHERS, 
& PUBLIC LIBRARIES: 
AN UPDATE

David Caron, Co-Publisher, ECW 
Press; Michael Ciccone, Director 
of Collections, Hamilton PL; 
Christina de Castell, Director, 
Resources & Technology, 
Vancouver PL; Tricia McCraney, 
Project Director, CULC/CBUC–
eBound Collaboration 

Since 2011, CULC/CBUC has 
been working directly with 
Canadian publishers to improve 
access to Canadian ebook 
content and introduce new 
models for public libraries. 
We discontinued our platform 
pilot project late in 2013 and 
redirected effort to continue 
work toward our goals of 
improving access to Canadian 
ebooks in Canadian public 
libraries, providing integrated 
solutions for borrowing, and 
building a direct and mutually 
beneficial relationship with 
Canadian publishers. Learn 
about library, consumer, and 
publishing trends that inform 
CULC/CBUC’s work and 
perspectives from task force 
members.

COLLECTIONHQ: HARNESSING EVIDENCE TO 
IMPROVE COLLECTION PERFORMANCE

Brendan Pearce, Business Development Manager, collectionHQ 

collectionHQ is the world’s leading collection performance 
improvement solution that helps librarians save time, save money, 
and improve their collection for customers. Based on real library 

case studies, this presentation will demonstrate how collectionHQ 
delivers action plans based on evidence to help libraries accurately 
and efficiently select, manage, and promote their collection. The 
demonstration will deliver an overview of the key evidence-based 
modules within the collectionHQ toolset including spending 
plans, ebook analysis, geographical circulation trends, and more.

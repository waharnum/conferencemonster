PROVINCIAL INTER-MINISTERIAL 
PUBLIC LIBRARY DISCUSSION FORUM

Facilitator: Wendy Newman, 
Senior Fellow, Faculty of 
Information, University of Toronto
Speakers from various Ministries 
will join AMPLO members 
and interested delegates.

This will be a facilitated 
discussion about common 
themes and interests 
between provincial ministries 
and public libraries. Using 
examples of successful past 
collaborations this will be a 
chance to share information 
and to identify opportunities 
for future partnerships. 
Discuss how the existing 
infrastructure of the public 
library sector, including staff 
expertise and experience, 
and existing customer base, 
provides a logical conduit to 
assist in reaching government 
goals and objectives. 
Objectives include informing 
staff in key ministries about 

existing and potential 
partnership opportunities and 
providing information about 
government goals, policies, 
and programs to consider 
how public libraries and 
government ministries can 
work together.
Co-hosted and co-sponsored 
by AMPLO (Administrators of 
Medium-sized Public Libraries 
in Ontario) and MTCS 
(Ministry of Tourism, Culture, 
& Sport).
Please note: pre-registration 
was required for this session. 
Registered participants have 
all received a confirmation 
email. Onsite registration is 
not available.
